LOCANDA 0.4 - CHANGELOG FROM 0.3



-Housed management. It is now possible to specify the guests, at the check-in, for each booking

-Export features. Locanda allows the export of housed guests and related data in a defined text format, in compliance with Italian rules about communication of touristic information for public security and statistical purposes
